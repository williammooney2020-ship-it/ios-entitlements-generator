iOS Entitlements Generator

Pick the capabilities your app needs and get a ready-to-use .entitlements plist. Drop it in Xcode alongside your provisioning profile — no manual XML editing.

ℹ️ After adding your .entitlements file, enable the matching capabilities in Xcode → [Target] → Signing & Capabilities. The entitlements file and the provisioning profile must match.

Select Capabilities

Check every capability your app uses. Where options appear, configure them for your setup.

Your App.entitlements

In Xcode: select your target → Signing & Capabilities → make sure your bundle ID matches. Then drag App.entitlements into your project (add to target). Xcode will detect it automatically if it has the same base name as your target, or set the path in Build Settings → CODE_SIGN_ENTITLEMENTS.
